Insurance Information

We accept most major private and public insurance plans.

While you may see your insurance name listed here, it is not a guarantee of coverage with each individual plan offered. All patients are encouraged to verify their coverage for naturopathic services by calling their insurance company directly to see if naturopaths are covered under their plan as well as confirm our doctors specifically are in network with the plan.
