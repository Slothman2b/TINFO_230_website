Our Practice


Vision

A joyful community that thrives on the health and resilience of all its people.


Mission

We provide exceptional and accessible primary healthcare to

South Puget Sound residents, blending the best of natural

and conventional medicine in an evidence-based approach.


We practice medicine with a philosophy that centers the patient’s values and experience, and pursues racial and social health justice.

We believe healthy individuals create a healthy community and we partner with patients to care for themselves while being cared for.

More Information

We optimize primary care by combining conventional and natural medicine to maximize benefit while reducing harm. Our practice is guided by scientific evidence and by personal connection. We take joy in caring for patients of all body sizes, abilities, ages, races, and genders.
