Insurance We Accept


To ensure we are accessible through your health insurance, we maintain contracts with most major private and public insurance companies.

While you may see your insurance listed here, it is not a guarantee of contract with every individual plan offered. You will ultimately be held responsible to pay for your doctor visit if it’s not covered by insurance.

Please avoid discovering this the hard way!

All patients are encouraged to verify their coverage for naturopathic services by calling their insurance company directly to see if naturopaths are covered under their plan and to confirm our doctors are in network.

If your insurance company is not listed here, please call our office to learn about our out-of-pocket options and discounts for those who are eligible.
