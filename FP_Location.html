Location and Parking


Patient Information

Location

107 N Tacoma Avenue
Tacoma WA 98403

Link Community Clinic is located in the heart of the Stadium District. Our blue-grey building is a bit recessed with a black iron fence in front of the property (you will enter through the open gate). We are nestled in between Platinum Salon and Open Arts

Parking

We offer very limited parking behind our building, If you are able to park behind the clinic, go to the backdoor with our sign on it and ring the doorbell for a staff member to come let you in. We always recommend allowing ample time to look for street parking.

The Link Rail is Here!

The Link Light Rail is up and running with stops passing through the Stadium District. Patients can now use the T-line to get to the clinic with hassle-free parking and a quick ride on the rail. Go to Sound Transit.org to find a schedule and plan your trip. With over 2,000 parking spaces available for Sound Transit passengers, patients are sure to find easy parking and make it to the train in no time. Patrons riding the link can park their personal vehicles for up to 24 hours in the Tacoma Dome Station parking garage without penalty. The average time frame for the link to travel from the Tacoma Dome Station to the Stadium District Stop is 15 minutes, with the train running through every 12 minutes.
